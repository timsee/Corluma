The ListCollectionWidget class is parent class for widgets shown on the lights page. When displaying either devices or moods, they are grouped by collections. Collections are a series of devices that can be controlled together. In the case of the devices page, each collection shows all its devices. On the moods page, each collection shows all the moods that use the devices in the collection.
