cor::Room Class Reference

The Room class is a derived class of a group and is a special case. No single light can belong to more than one room. It may also contain a list of subgroups, referencable by their unique ID. A subgroup points to the unique ID of another valid group, where all lights within the subgroup are also part of the main group. More...
